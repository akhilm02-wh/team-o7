The Cosmic Roadmap

Our Vision Forward

The stars have aligned. Our path stretches beyond the horizon — here is what the seers have foretold for Team O7.

Phase I · Now
Establish the Foundation
Complete kisansathi.in MVP. Ship Invoice Fraud Detector v1. Build internal tooling and CI/CD pipelines.
